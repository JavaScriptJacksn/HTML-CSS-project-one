Who Are We?

We are a small community of the toughest and most raw powerlifting, bodybuilding and crossfit enthusiasts making a name for ourselves in the world of power and strength.

Our Staff, and what they bring to the gym, in their own words:

Robert Mignot

I'm classically trained powerlifter and coach who likes to take his lifting apprentices through the old school methodology and psychology of making the weight move. I love a minimalist approach with less fuss of technicalities in regards to powerlifting regulations and we DO NOT CUT CORNERS.

Stavros Panayi

Whilst I still adhere to the gym's ethos and sport's professional conventions, I specialize in the inverse of my fellow coach Bob. He takes things to the extreme in strength where I focus more on the techniques to add more weight to your lifts in strict accordance with the International Powerlifting Federation, allowing you UNLEASH YOUR FULL POTENTIAL, essentially learning how to max-out in competitions safely. It's not as simple as 'cutting corners'.

Teresa Gold

I'm the resident specialist in physiotherapy and body maintenance. Powerlifting, bodybuilding and crossfit can all be VERY DANGEROUS SPORTS. It's my job to ensure all measures possible are taken by our athletes to prevent injury, and when they may occur, to implement proper physiotherapeutic programs to ensure no permanent damage is done, and that they may continue lifting as soon as possible

What do we do?

We provide the necessities for primarily experienced lifters looking to further themselves in their goals of strength, bodily function and health through a social community both in and out of the gym. Our level of performance in our community is second-to-none, so therefore we urge all new members to consider the extremely physically and mentally demanding nature of our sports and that we only encourage fairly experienced individuals in the gym to join us for more specialist training.

What we provide:

  • Qualified and certified to industry regulation powerlifting and crossfit coaching
  • Regular meetups and competitions under IFP and Crossfit regulations
  • Specialist training equipment
  • Our 'Apprentice program' A One-to-one personal training scheme focused around long term trainer-althlete bonding and relationship growth (only for those serious about competeing professionally).
  • Post and pre-workout physiotherapy with our specialists
  • One of a kind social community of like-minded lifters and enthusiats in the sports

Why do we do it?

The Art of War strives to be social, a place where bonds are formed unlike any other, through blood and sweat.

Our Ethos is simple, once our athletes set foot into our gym, they forget the outside world and put their heart and body into their training for an hour or two completely removed from their troubles or triumphs outside of the gym. For the duration of their training they unleash their potential and put in the work day in, day out with a true passion for what our gym stands for.
